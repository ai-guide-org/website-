ğŸ“ Tips for Writing Good Prompts:

  1. Start with a clear goal - what do you want?

ğŸš€ Advanced Techniques:

1. Role Assignment

"Imagine you are a [role]"

Example: "Imagine you are a senior programmer with 10 years of Python experience. Explain to me..."

Other roles: teacher, business consultant, editor, marketer, designer.

2. Step-by-Step Thinking (Chain-of-Thought)

"Explain step by step how to..."

Example: "Explain step by step how to create a website with WordPress"

AI will break the task into logical steps.

3. Giving Examples (Few-shot prompting)

"Here's an example of how I want you to respond..."

Example: "Here's an example of a good response: [example]. Now respond to: [your question]"

4. Iterative Improvement

"This is good, but can you..."

1. Give initial request
2. See the result
3. Provide feedback
4. Request improvements
